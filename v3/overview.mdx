<Info>
  **Preview.** v3 is the next version of the VerifyYou API. It is not yet
  generally available — shapes and field names may change before launch. This
  section describes the intended flow so you can plan your integration early.
</Info>

v2 gives you two calls to verify a real, unique human. v3 keeps that simplicity and adds reusable configs, white-label branding, and a per-user enrollment step — so it fits both a single product and a platform serving many companies.

## Two ways to integrate

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Verify for your product" icon="user-check">
    You're verifying people for **your** product. Set up one verification, send users to its link, and read the result.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build into your platform" icon="layer-group">
    You serve **multiple companies**. Programmatically create a verification per company, bind each one's branding with the asset API, and group work under your own tenant ids.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Concepts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Verification" icon="gear">
    A reusable config — redirect URL, an attached asset, and the rules a person must satisfy. Create it **once** and reuse its link for everyone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Confirmation" icon="circle-check">
    One person's result. The hosted flow returns a confirmation token in the redirect; you read it to learn whether they're verified.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Asset" icon="palette">
    A reusable white-label bundle — company name, logo, banner, font. One asset can brand many verifications. (Platform feature.)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Uniqueness pool" icon="fingerprint">
    The set a verified human is deduped against. Account-backed people are unique forever; anonymous ones reset monthly. See [Uniqueness](/v3/uniqueness).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How the flow fits together

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a verification">
    Define your rules once with `POST /v3/verification/create`. You get back a reusable `verification_url`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send users to it">
    Share that link with anyone you want to verify. Optionally [enroll](/v3/verifications#enroll-a-user) a known user first to pre-attach their data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We verify them">
    The user completes the hosted flow (phone check → liveness + uniqueness). We redirect back to your `redirect_url` with `vy_confirmation` and `vy_status`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the confirmation">
    Confirm the result server-side with `GET /v3/confirmations/{confirmation}` using the token from the redirect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Consume it (optional)">
    If the verification was `consumable`, call `POST /v3/confirmations/{confirmation}/consume` once the user finishes the gated action — they can't come back through.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Coming from v2? Your two-call integration keeps working. v3 is additive —
  adopt the pieces you need.
</Note>
