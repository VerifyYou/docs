## Why it is so much cheaper than KYC

A full KYC check runs a dollar or more per person, because it collects and verifies a government identity. HumanCheck proves a real, unique, live human without any of that, so it lands at about one to two cents, often fifty to a hundred times less. The right way to compare is cost per usable, real respondent, rather than a tax on every check.
